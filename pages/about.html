Who am I?

If you are interested in knowing more about me, this is the correct page!

My name is Oliva Ros, and I am a food photographer, critic, and passionate traveler. I have more than five years of experience taking pictures of food and reviewing them, so now I am considered an expert in the area. One proof of that is the impact that any of my comments cause in social media and restaurants whenever I talk about food.

I started my career as a food enthusiast when I was still in college. During that time, I decided to get to know all the restaurants in my State, take pictures of their food, and sample them, which I was able to accomplish in no more than three months. Then I decided to have eaten in all the restaurants in the USA by the end of the year, which I achieved before December 30th. By that time, I was already being recognized as an expert food photographer and critic. So, the next part of my plan was to eat in all the restaurants on earth, and that is what I am still trying to do.

In all my travels, I have been helping restaurant owners to create more compelling menus and take good pictures of their food. If you feel like your food is not worth the effort, I can assure you that I have been able to make dog food look like a dish from a Michelin star restaurant, so there is always hope for everyone! Do not hesitate to contact me! I will be more than pleased to help you!
